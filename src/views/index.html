Products

SubVT (Substrate Validator Toolkit) is a native mobile application for iOS and Android phones, tablets and wearables that provides node operators with tools that aid them in running their validators on any Substrate-based blockchain network.

Development of SubVT is proudly supported by the Kusama Treasury through on-chain motions #426 and #326.

subvt.com

1KV Telegram Bot

A telegram bot for the validators enrolled in the Polkadot and Kusama Thousand Validators (1KV) Programme. You may find Polkadot bot available for chat here , and the Kusama bot here .

1KV Telegram bot is currently being upgraded to serve all validators of Polkadot and Kusama with increased functionality, an effort proudly supported by the Web3 Foundation .

Kusama bot Polkadot bot

ChainViz

ChainViz is a real-time 3D chain visualization web application for Kusama.

ChainViz is a work in progress, and the currently released alpha version visualizes in real-time the block production process, network status and validators statistics of the Kusama network.
